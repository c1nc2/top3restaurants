Top 3 Restaurants Around TIU

Sho Last Updated: May 28th

1.

Ebitonnkotu-Ramen

〒350-1103 Saitama, Kawagoe, Kasumigasekihigashi, 1 Chome-2-21

The ramen restaurant is in front of the station. This restaurant is new to the town. Although, it's a little expensive, the quality of the food is good. A good place to go with friends after class.

Recommended: Milk Ramen w/ Rice

2.

Gusto

〒350-1101 Matoba 2243-1, Kawagoe, Saitama, Japan

It's a family restaurant, a good place for students since it's affordable, and a good atmosphere if you want to study there as well. It's also located close to the station and the university. Pretty good to have lunch on a Sunday.

Recommended: チーズINハンバーガー(Cheese IN Hamburger) N.103

3.

Matsuya

〒350-1103 Kasumigasekihigashi 1 Chome-2-22, Kawagoe, Saitama

Perfect for students at TIU who wants a quick lunch or dinner for cheap. Sometimes the local is busy, and you'll have to eat around other people. The service is fast, so you won't have to wait for more than other restaurants.

Recommended: Brown Sauce Hamburger Patty Set
